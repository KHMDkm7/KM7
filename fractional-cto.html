When to Bring in a Fractional CTO vs Hiring Full-Time

← Back to Home

Many startup founders assume they need a full-time CTO as soon as they begin building a product. In reality, hiring a senior executive too early can increase costs and reduce flexibility.

A fractional CTO provides strategic technical leadership without the long-term commitment and salary of a full-time executive. This allows founders to validate ideas, build MVPs, and make key technical decisions while keeping burn rates under control.

For early-stage startups, this model can be especially valuable when product requirements are still evolving and engineering teams are small.

As startups grow and technical complexity increases, founders can then evaluate whether a full-time CTO is needed.
